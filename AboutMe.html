About Me

For as long as I remember, I've been interested in gaming and art. Once I finished High School, I thought to combine my interests into one and pursue a career in Game Art.

In 2020, unaware of what the next few years hold, I started my 3D journey at Academy of Interactive Entertainment (AIE). After two years, in and out of lockdowns, I developed my skills, helped produce several games, and graduated as one of three finalists for Outstanding Artist of AIE for 2021.

During my time at AIE I've had the pleasure to work with some amazing people that have helped me develop my skills as an artist, as a team member, and most importantly, myself. I've produced multiple games, all on a small indie scale, however that has allowed me to experience game development and expand my abilities.
