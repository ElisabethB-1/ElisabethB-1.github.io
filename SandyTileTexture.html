Stylised Ruin
Tiles

This Ruined Tiles were found deep in the forest, in a temple long forgotten,
or maybe its located with in an anicent desert ruins that the sands have long since claimed within its dunes.

A material I made while following a tutorial of Alysson Soares (https://www.artstation.com/karalysson)

The original textures used sand to submerge the tiles but for the project I'm planning to use it for is set in a forest ruins (Forgotten Ruins), so I needed to adjust it to be more grassy or mossy.

I created a simple mossy texture that can be swapped for the sand. By just changing two nodes, i can swap between the sand and moss versions.
